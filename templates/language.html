Plants - Language Selection

Tulsi

Medicinal Name: Ocimum sanctum

Uses: Treats cough, cold, and boosts immunity.

Habitat: Grows widely in tropical and subtropical regions. It is commonly found in home gardens, temples, and rural areas in India.

Aloe Vera

Medicinal Name: Aloe barbadensis miller

Uses: Soothes burns, improves digestion, and hydrates skin.

Habitat: Native to arid regions. Aloe Vera prefers sandy, well-drained soil and is commonly found in dry, tropical, and subtropical climates.

Rosa damascena

Medicinal Name: Rosa damascena

Uses: Acts as a coolant, relieves stress, and improves skin health.

Habitat: Grows in temperate and subtropical climates. It is typically cultivated in regions with cool temperatures and well-drained, loamy soil.
